# Hello World — your first trip through the loop

There is almost nothing to build here — one button that says hello. That's the
point. Follow the trail top to bottom — a Decision, a Plan, a Scope, two
Tasks — and each one tells you what it is and what to do next.

By the end you'll have driven the whole loop once:
approve → commit → claim → submit → gate.

Start in the Inbox and approve the decision. Then open this project's board.
